Communicating information through heatmaps

When you have lots of measurements for lots of samples, heatmaps can be a great way to communicate the patterns you have observed. And if you have additional information about your samples, e.g. treatment groups, genotypes, age, etc, the ComplexHeatmap package offers options to display them at the same time.
